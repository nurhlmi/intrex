Unacceptable Goods


We do not ship the following:

  • Alcohol
  • Artwork or antiques
  • Cash or currency or negotiable instruments in bearer form (like cheques, activated credit cards, money orders, promissory notes, travellers cheques, bankers drafts, bonds, open tickets)
  • Dangerous or hazardous goods and materials
  • Human remains including ashes
  • Living creatures, plants and products (eg. animal skin, fur, poultry, meat, produce and tobacco)
  • Narcotic substances
  • Perishable goods
  • Pornography
  • Precious metals and stones
  • Weapons, firearms and ammunition
  • Goods requiring special license without prior arrangements being made
  • Goods that may cause damage or delay to other goods
  • Goods packaged likely to cause injury to any person
  • Goods inadequately packaged
  • Packages that are wet or leaking
  • Dangerous goods may only be accepted and carried in accordance with the current International Air Transport Association (IATA) Dangerous Goods Regulations.
  • Any items prohibited by any law, regulation or statute of any country to or through which the items may be shipped.
